The Maison

Elihav Sasson

For nearly two decades, Elihav Sasson has designed modest bridal and evening couture from his atelier in Neve Tzedek, Tel Aviv. Each piece is a study in restraint — long sleeves, considered necklines, and hand-finished detail that lets the woman, not the garment, take presence.

Every gown begins with a private consultation and is made entirely to measure.
